📌 Frequently Asked Questions — Contact & Care Support

❓ How do I schedule a wellness exam?

You can use the contact form above or call (512) 555-7890. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for routine exams. New patients receive a welcome package with a preventive care plan tailored to your pet’s age and lifestyle.

🚑 When should I use the emergency hotline?

If your pet shows signs of difficulty breathing, uncontrolled bleeding, seizure, bloated/distended abdomen, or hit by car — call (512) 555-0199 immediately. Our triage team will guide you and prepare the emergency suite.

💰 Do you accept pet insurance?

Yes, we work with Trupanion, Healthy Paws, Embrace, and most major insurers. We also provide itemized superbills for direct reimbursement. Please bring your policy details to your appointment.

📄 Can I request prescription refills online?

Absolutely. Use our contact form or email refills@keenanimalcare.com with your pet’s name and medication. Please allow 48 hours for processing. Heartworm preventatives can be shipped directly to your home.

🕊️ Do you offer end-of-life and hospice care?

Our compassionate team supports palliative care, quality-of-life assessments, and in-home euthanasia services upon request. Contact our care coordinator to discuss options with kindness and dignity.

🧾 What if I have a billing question?

Our front desk staff can explain estimates, deposits, and payment plans. For detailed inquiries, email billing@keenanimalcare.com or call during business hours.

🐾 Additional Pet Care Resources & Veterinary Hotlines

Keen Animal Care partners with local shelters and rescue groups. If you've found a stray animal, we offer free microchip scanning. For poison emergencies, ASPCA Animal Poison Control Center (APCC) is available 24/7: (888) 426-4435 (fees may apply). We also maintain a list of 24-hour specialty referral hospitals in the Austin area — ask our reception desk for updated details.

🩺 Before your visit: Bring any prior medical records, a fresh fecal sample (if possible), and list of current medications. For cats, use a secure carrier with familiar bedding. For nervous dogs, consider an Adaptil collar or ask us about fear-free techniques. Our clinic is certified “Low-Stress Handling” — we take extra time to acclimate anxious pets.

📣 Volunteer & Community: Keen Animal Care hosts quarterly low-cost vaccination clinics and monthly pet loss support groups. Reach out via the contact form to register or volunteer. We believe in giving back to the furry community that enriches our lives.

💬 Telehealth Consultations

Not every issue requires a clinic visit. We offer virtual consultations for behavioral concerns, nutrition advice, dermatology follow-ups, and post-op checks. Telehealth appointments are $45 and available Monday–Friday. Please fill out the form and mention “telehealth” in the message, and our team will send you a secure video link. Please note that state regulations require an existing veterinary-client-patient relationship for prescription medications, but new clients can still book a virtual consult for general guidance.

🌎 International Inquiries & Relocation Support

Planning to move abroad with your pet? Our travel consultation service covers USDA endorsement, health certificates, microchip requirements, and quarantine regulations for over 30 countries. Schedule a dedicated “pet travel appointment” via our contact form or call our front desk. We ensure all documentation is processed correctly to avoid travel delays.

Note: due to the volume of messages, we prioritize urgent cases. Non-urgent inquiries receive a response within 2 business days. Please include your pet’s name and your preferred contact method for faster assistance.

🏆 Awards & Accreditations

Keen Animal Care is an AAHA-accredited hospital (American Animal Hospital Association) since 2018, meaning we meet rigorous standards for equipment, pain management, and medical record keeping. We’re also a Cat-Friendly Practice certified by the American Association of Feline Practitioners — our exam rooms have feline pheromone diffusers and soft bedding to reduce stress. Our veterinarians regularly attend conferences on integrative medicine, dentistry, and ultrasound. Trust your companions to a team that prioritizes continuing education and compassionate science.

🧸 Donations & Wishlist

Do you want to support pets in need? Our clinic accepts unopened food, clean towels, gently used crates, and monetary donations for the “Keen Care Fund” — which helps low-income families with emergency surgeries. Contact our community outreach coordinator via the form with subject “Donation”. Every contribution directly saves lives.
