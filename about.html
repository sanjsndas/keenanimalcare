Our story, mission & heart

🐕 Who we are

Founded in 2012, Keen Animal Care began as a small, family-run practice with a dream: to create a sanctuary where pets receive not only medical excellence but also empathy and respect. Over a decade later, we have grown into a trusted wellness hub serving thousands of furry, feathered, and scaled family members across Texas and beyond. Our philosophy fuses modern veterinary science with a gentle, holistic touch — because every animal deserves a stress-free, joyful healing experience.

We are a team of passionate veterinarians, certified technicians, behaviorists, and support staff who treat each patient like our own. From wellness exams to advanced surgery, dental care to nutritional counseling, we adapt our approach to your pet’s unique personality. At Keen Animal Care, we never rush an appointment; we listen, observe, and build lasting bonds.

📖 The Keen philosophy: beyond conventional care

🏥 A fear-free & inclusive environment

We understand that a trip to the vet can be overwhelming. That’s why our clinic was designed with separate cat-only waiting zones, calming pheromone diffusers, non-slip surfaces, and soft music. Each exam room has cozy bedding and treats to create positive associations. Our team is trained in low-stress handling: we let pets set the pace, using gentle restraint only when necessary. Since implementing fear-free protocols, we've seen reduced sedation needs and more relaxed visits.

🌿 Integrative medicine & holistic options

While we embrace cutting-edge diagnostics (digital radiology, ultrasound, in-house lab), we also offer integrative therapies: therapeutic laser for arthritis, acupuncture for chronic pain, herbal supplements (under veterinary supervision), and nutritional consults with homemade diet planning. For pets with cancer, we collaborate with oncology specialists and discuss palliative care options that prioritize quality of life. Our pharmacy stocks holistic remedies alongside conventional meds — because the best plan is individualized.

🐶 Canine rehabilitation & wellness programs

In 2019, we opened our rehabilitation wing featuring an underwater treadmill, therapeutic exercises, and a certified canine rehabilitation practitioner. Dogs recovering from TPLO surgery, senior pets with hip dysplasia, and even agility athletes benefit from customized conditioning plans. We combine manual therapy, stretching, and targeted exercises to restore mobility without pain. Our weight management club helps overweight pets shed pounds safely, reducing diabetes and joint strain.

💡 Community outreach & education initiatives

We believe that accessible education transforms animal welfare. Each month we host “Pet First Aid & CPR” workshops, virtual Q&A sessions on pet nutrition, and free seminars about senior pet care. Our “Keen Kindness” program provides low-cost spay/neuter for rescue animals and partners with local food banks to supply pet food for families in need. Since 2018, we’ve sponsored over 1,200 adoption events, helping shelter animals find loving homes. We also offer veterinary shadowing opportunities for aspiring DVM students from local universities.

Keen Animal Care has been voted “Best Veterinary Clinic in Austin” for four consecutive years (2021–2024) by the Austin Pet Chronicle. We are a certified Cat-Friendly Practice by the American Association of Feline Practitioners and a Fear-Free Certified Clinic. 📚 Deep dives: Our ethical standards & veterinary innovation

🔬 The science of pain management

At Kean Animal Care, we stay ahead of veterinary analgesia. We utilize multi-modal protocols including local blocks, NSAIDs, gabapentin, and adjunctive therapies like cold laser and acupuncture. Our surgical patients receive pre-emptive pain relief, ensuring they wake up comfortable. Chronic pain patients are treated with a combination of joint supplements (omega-3, adequan), physical therapy, and weight control. We also provide owner education on recognizing subtle pain signs: decreased activity, changes in sleeping position, or reluctance to jump.

🧬 Genetic testing & personalized medicine

To offer tailored preventive care, we partner with genetic testing labs to identify breed-specific risks (e.g., MDR1 mutation in herding breeds, PRA in certain dogs). This allows us to adjust medication protocols and screen for diseases early. For cats, we recommend DNA tests for HCM (hypertrophic cardiomyopathy) risk in Maine Coons and Ragdolls. We believe personalized medicine is the future — and we are proud to bring it to everyday practice.

🩺 Advanced dentistry: more than cleanings

Our dental suite includes high-resolution intraoral radiographs that reveal hidden pathology below the gumline. We perform root canals, extractions with regional blocks, and periodontal therapies. Dental disease is linked to heart, liver, and kidney damage, so thorough oral care is a cornerstone of wellness. We also offer non-anesthetic dental cleanings for cooperative pets (only mild tartar) but always recommend full oral health assessment under anesthesia for complete evaluation.

🌎 Sustainability in practice

Keen Animal Care was the first clinic in our region to switch to anesthetic gas capture systems, reducing greenhouse emissions. We use sterilizable surgical wraps instead of disposables, recycle pharmaceutical blister packs, and compost biodegradable waste. Our building is solar-powered, and we promote eco-friendly pet products (biodegradable waste bags, sustainable toys) in our retail section. Small changes, huge impact.
