🍪 Cookies & Similar Technologies

Transparent, simple, and respectful — understand how we use cookies to improve your experience on Keen Animal Care website.

🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, enhance site performance, analyze traffic, and provide essential features such as form submissions and secure login. Cookies do not harm your device and cannot access personal files. This Cookies Policy explains the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

🐾 Our Commitment: At Keen Animal Care, we believe in total transparency. We never use cookies to collect sensitive medical or payment information. Any data collected via cookies is anonymized where possible, and we never sell cookie data to third parties.

📊 Types of Cookies We Use

Based on function and purpose, we classify cookies into four main categories. Below is a detailed breakdown:

Cookie Category Purpose Examples & Lifespan
Strictly Necessary Essential for website functionality. Without these, services like navigation, appointment forms, or security features cannot work. Session cookies (expire after browser close), authentication cookies (24h).
Examples: PHPSESSID, csrf_token.
Performance & Analytics Collect anonymous data about how visitors interact with our site — which pages are most popular, load times, errors. Helps us improve user experience. Persistent (up to 2 years).
Examples: Google Analytics (_ga, _gid), Hotjar.
Functional Remember user choices (language, region, font size, cookie consent preferences) to offer personalized features. Persistent (1 year).
Examples: language_pref, cookie_consent_status.
Targeting / Advertising We currently DO NOT use third-party advertising cookies for retargeting or behavioral ads. However, if we partner with pet wellness campaigns, we will update this policy and seek explicit consent. Not actively used — reserved only with opt-in.

🔧 Cookie Management & Your Consent

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner appears, giving you control over non-essential cookies. You can accept all, reject optional cookies (except strictly necessary ones), or customize your preferences. Your choice is saved via a functional cookie. You may change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer of our website or via your browser settings.

🛠️ How to Disable or Delete Cookies in Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can block all cookies (including essential ones) but note that some website features may break (e.g., online appointment request forms). Here’s how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Third-party cookies → Block or allow cookies.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Manage exceptions.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Block all cookies (or manage per site).
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies.
  • Mobile devices: Refer to your device's settings under “Browser” or “Privacy.”

For further guidance, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

📈 Third-Party Analytics Providers

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity (only if enabled by your consent) to understand how users engage with our website. These services place cookies that generate pseudonymous usage data. IP addresses are anonymized before storage. For Google Analytics opt-out, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. We also use internal tracking to monitor form abandonment and improve our service — no personal pet data is attached.

✨ Do Not Track Signals: Our website respects the "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting if enabled in your browser. If DNT is active, we will disable non-essential analytics and marketing cookies automatically. However, strictly necessary cookies remain functional for core website operation.

⏳ Cookie Retention Periods

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies have varying lifespans: analytics cookies may last up to 24 months, while functional preference cookies are typically stored for 12 months. We do not use flash cookies (Local Shared Objects) or ever-lasting supercookies. You can manually clear all stored cookies at any time through browser settings.

🔐 How We Protect Cookie Data

Cookie information is transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connections. Any analytics data we collect is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users or specific pets. We do not cross-reference cookie data with medical records or personal identifiers. Security measures include regular scans for vulnerabilities and access restrictions to analytics dashboards.

🍪 First-Party vs. Third-Party Cookies

All cookies served on our domain (keenanimalcare.com) are first-party cookies, set by us. We do not embed third-party tracking cookies from unknown networks. However, our embedded video content (e.g., pet care tutorials from YouTube) may set third-party cookies if you interact with the video player. You can block third-party cookies via browser settings without affecting core site functions.
